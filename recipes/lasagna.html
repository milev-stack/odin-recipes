Lasagna

How to Make

Although you can tailor the specifics of this recipe to suit your needs and tastes, these are the ingredients that should serve as a good starting point :

Ingredients

  • Meat: If you're looking for a delicious meat filled lasagna, consider sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Spices and seasonings: This recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Use store bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
